Lasagna

Description

This popular lasagna is made with a simple meat-sauce base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is excellent at feeding a large family, freezes well, and makes for an easy weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (14 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon to 1/4 cup sugar (to taste, optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. assemble the lasagna
  3. wait for oven to preheat
  4. put assembled lasagna in preheated oven
  5. bake for 35 min or until cheese bubbles
  6. take out and let sit for 5 min
  7. Enjoy!
