Pizza

Description

This popular pizza recipe is quick and easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 packet of fast rise yeast
  • Sauce base of your choosing
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 cup of water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. assemble the pizza
  3. wait for oven to preheat
  4. put assembled Pizza in preheated oven
  5. bake for 35 min or until cheese bubbles
  6. take out and let sit for 5 min
  7. Enjoy!
